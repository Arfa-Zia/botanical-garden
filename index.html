Some Unique Flowers of our Garden

Water lilies

Water lilies are freshwater plants.There are about 70 species of water lilies. They are native to the temperate and tropical parts of the world.Water lilies are found in the Americas, Europe, Asia, Africa and Australia.

Tulip

Tulips are genus of about 100 species of bulbous herbs, native to Central Asia and Turkey. Tulips are among the most popular of all garden flowers, and numerous cultivars and varieties have been develope.

Dahlia

Dahlias have been a floral favorite for generations and it's easy to see why. Their bright blooms, vast variation, and heart-warming history make these flowers special in many ways. There are currently 42 species of Dahlia.

Jasmine

Jasmine is a genus of shrubs and vines in the olive family. It contains around 200 species native to tropical and warm temperate regions of Eurasia, Africa, and Oceania. Jasmines are widely cultivated for the characteristic fragrance of their flowers.
